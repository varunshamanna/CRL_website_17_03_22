Who We Are?

Central Research Laboratory, KIMS is a laboratory-based, translational and clinical research at KIMS, one of the renowned medical institution in Karnataka, India. We arededicated for conducting research at advanced level as well as for diagnosis and infectious diseases research.

This facility is extensively used by our staff for completing their External and Internal Research funding projects, postgraduates for completing their thesis/research projects as well as by undergraduates to complete their STS ICMR projects and for any short-term research projects.

CRL acts as a single point of contact for investigators to accelerate their research study and provides structured environment where researchers, undergraduates, Postgraduates and clinicians can work together on projects that are relevant and fundamentally important

  • NIVER
  • GHRU-India unit
  • ICMR approved NABL accredited COVID-19 testing lab
  • World’s first GFGP certified laboratory
  • WHONET nodal cente

CRL has the honor of being the only Centre in India recognized and authorized to evaluate the efficacy of pneumococcal vaccines using ELISA and Opsonophagocytic assay. It is also a reference Centre for Pneumococcal serotyping in India. CRL is one of the world’s first laboratories to be bestowed Good Financial Grant Practices (GFGP). It is also a WHONET nodal Centre with special focus on the analysis of antimicrobial susceptibility test.

The center has built and nurtured collaborations with academic institutions and hospitals across the country and provides training, research support and referral service in the field of Microbiology.

It also houses ICMR approved NABL accredited COVID-19 testing lab one of the first to start in the vicinity and has processed more than 1.5 lac samples.

CRL has joined hands with many sponsors like Pfizer India, Sanofi Pasteur India, ICMR (Govt. of India), BIRAC (Govt. of India) and NIHR, UK. with their projects and have been successfully completed while some are ongoing
