Dr. Geetha Nagaraj

Associate Professor

OrciD: 0000-0001-8099-7493

Expertise

Dr. Geetha is a senior researcher with a Doctorate in Allied Health Sciences (Microbiology) and 17yrs of research experience in Genomics, molecular biology, microbiology, proteomics, bioinformatics, immunology, Sales & Marketing, and Administration. She was a guest researcher at CDC, Atlanta, USA, Dr. Moon Nahm lab, University of Alabama, Birmingham, USA, and Dr. David Aanensen lab, cGPS, Wellcome Sanger Institute. She has fourteen publications in peer-reviewed journals to her credit and has presented her research as oral presentations in international conferences like ECCMID, ESPID, ISPPD, Gulf Thoracic, and WSPID. She was associated with multicenter, population, and laboratory-based surveillance studies. She was actively involved in the implementation of ISO15189:2012 and ARS 1651:2018 standards at CRL, KIMS. She has undergone training in Quality management systems - ISO9001:2015, ISO15189:2012 IA &QMS, ARS 1651:2018, GCLP, and HACCP.
