Title: Evaluation of the antimicrobial efficacy and safety of PureHands as a hand sanitizer: Aprospective, double blind, randomized and placebo-controlled phase III clinical trial

Sponsoring agency The Himalaya Drug Company, India Pvt. Ltd

Project leader Dr K L Ravikumar, Prof & HOD Department of Microbiology

Year in which started 2004

Duration in months 12 months

Research Objectives

This study was planned to evaluate the clinical efficacy and safety of PureHands in ensuring hand hygiene in HCWs, in hospital settings.

Research outcomes

This study observed a significant reduction in the mean number of CFUs in the PureHands group, as compared to the placebo group on days 3, 5 and 7 and a non-significant reduction in the mean number of CFUs in the PureHands groups, as compared to the placebo groups on day 10.
