Research Facilities

Research Laboratory facilities at Central Research Laboratory (CRL), KIMS, Bengaluru

Research at CRL focuses on developing new methodologies, technologies and processes. Scientists are supported by state-of-the-art research tools and scientific platforms. The laboratory has a well-designed and equipped furnished sections housing Biosafety level 2 and level 3 facility, Genomics, Microbiology, Immunology and Bioinformatics sections separately. Administrative block of CRL consists of Data management section, Office and Accounts section, Stores, Seminar hall and Cafeteria.
